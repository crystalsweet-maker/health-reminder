Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

English Version

Last Updated: April 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Health Reminder browser extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how your data is handled.

Information We Collect

The Health Reminder extension collects and stores the following information:

  • User Preferences: Settings you configure for reminders, including reminder intervals, sound preferences, and notification settings.
  • Usage Statistics: Basic usage data such as the number of reminders triggered, which is stored locally on your device.
  • Subscription Information: If you subscribe to our Pro version, we store your subscription status and expiration date.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the core functionality of the extension
  • To save your preferences and settings
  • To improve and optimize the extension's performance
  • To verify subscription status for Pro features

Data Storage

All your settings and usage data are stored locally on your device using browser storage mechanisms (localStorage and chrome.storage). This data does not leave your device except in the following circumstances:

  • If you subscribe to Pro features, subscription verification data is exchanged with our servers
  • If you explicitly choose to sync your settings across devices (if this feature is available)
  • If you make a donation, your donation status is stored locally and may be verified with our servers to unlock Pro features

Third-Party Services

The extension may use the following third-party services:

  • Payment processors for handling Pro subscriptions
  • Ko-fi platform for processing donations from international users

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy and data handling practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies. For Ko-fi donations, please refer to Ko-fi's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, you can either use the extension's built-in options to manage your data or contact us directly.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at healthreminder@outlook.com or visit our GitHub repository to submit an issue.

中文版本

最后更新：2025年4月

本隐私政策描述了健康提醒助手浏览器扩展（"我们"、"我们的"或"扩展"）在您使用我们的扩展时如何收集、使用和共享您的信息。我们致力于保护您的隐私并确保您了解您的数据是如何被处理的。

我们收集的信息

健康提醒助手扩展收集并存储以下信息：

  • 用户偏好：您为提醒配置的设置，包括提醒间隔、声音偏好和通知设置。
  • 使用统计：基本使用数据，如触发的提醒次数，这些数据存储在您的设备本地。
  • 订阅信息：如果您订阅了我们的专业版，我们会存储您的订阅状态和到期日期。

我们如何使用您的信息

我们将收集的信息用于以下目的：

  • 提供和维护扩展的核心功能
  • 保存您的偏好和设置
  • 改进和优化扩展的性能
  • 验证专业版功能的订阅状态

数据存储

您的所有设置和使用数据都使用浏览器存储机制（localStorage和chrome.storage）存储在您的设备本地。除以下情况外，这些数据不会离开您的设备：

  • 如果您订阅了专业版功能，订阅验证数据会与我们的服务器交换
  • 如果您明确选择在设备之间同步您的设置（如果此功能可用）
  • 如果您进行捐赠，您的捐赠状态会存储在本地，并可能与我们的服务器进行验证以解锁专业版功能

第三方服务

扩展可能使用以下第三方服务：

  • 处理专业版订阅的支付处理器
  • 用于处理国际用户捐赠的Ko-fi平台

每个第三方服务都有自己的隐私政策和数据处理实践。我们鼓励您查看他们的隐私政策。对于Ko-fi捐赠，请参阅Ko-fi的服务条款和隐私政策。

您的权利

您有权：

  • 访问我们持有的关于您的个人数据
  • 请求更正您的个人数据
  • 请求删除您的数据
  • 随时撤回同意

要行使这些权利，您可以使用扩展的内置选项来管理您的数据，或直接联系我们。

本隐私政策的变更

我们可能会不时更新我们的隐私政策。我们将通过在此页面上发布新的隐私政策并更新"最后更新"日期来通知您任何变更。

联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过healthreminder@outlook.com联系我们，或访问我们的GitHub仓库提交问题。
